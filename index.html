Biography

Florencia Cerutti is an Olympian and internationally recognized sailing coach with extensive experience in athlete development, high-performance coaching, and sailing program leadership.

Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Cerutti built her athletic career in Olympic single-handed classes and represented Paraguay at the 2008 Olympic Games in Beijing, becoming the first Paraguayan woman to compete in Olympic sailing.

Throughout her competitive career, she earned international podium finishes and established herself as a respected athlete in South American and international sailing circuits.

Following her Olympic campaign, Cerutti transitioned into coaching and leadership roles, working with national federations, elite training environments, and major sailing clubs across Europe and the United States.

Her professional work extends beyond on-the-water coaching. She has also led racing programs, developed long-term athlete pathways, supervised coaching staff, coordinated regatta logistics, supported fleet management, and helped sailing organizations grow participation and strengthen performance structures.

Cerutti currently serves as Head Coach and Assistant Sailing Director at Norfolk Yacht & Country Club in Virginia, where she works with youth development and competitive sailing programs.
