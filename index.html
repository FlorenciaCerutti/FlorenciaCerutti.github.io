Biography

Olympian. Coach. Program Builder.

Florencia Cerutti is an Olympian and internationally recognized sailing coach with extensive experience in high-performance athlete development, competitive team leadership, and sailing program growth.

Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Cerutti built her athletic career in Olympic single-handed classes and represented Paraguay at the 2008 Olympic Games in Beijing, becoming the first Paraguayan woman to compete in Olympic sailing.

Throughout her competitive career, she achieved important results in South American and international regattas, establishing herself as a respected athlete in elite racing environments.

After her Olympic campaign, she transitioned into coaching and leadership roles, working with clubs, federations, development programs, and high-performance sailing environments across Europe and the United States.

Her work extends beyond on-the-water coaching. Cerutti has also led long-term sailing programs, supported athlete pathways, supervised staff, coordinated regatta logistics, and contributed to sustainable growth within competitive sailing organizations.

She currently serves as Head Coach and Assistant Sailing Director at Norfolk Yacht & Country Club in Virginia.
