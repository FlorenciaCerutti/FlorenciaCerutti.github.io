Biography

About Flor Cerutti

Flor Cerutti is an Olympian and international sailing coach whose career connects Olympic-level competition with athlete development and the leadership of competitive sailing programs around the world.

Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, Cerutti developed her sailing career in Olympic single-handed dinghy classes and represented Paraguay at the 2008 Olympic Games in Beijing. Her participation marked a historic milestone as the first Paraguayan woman to compete in Olympic sailing.

Following her Olympic campaign, she established herself in Europe and began building an international coaching career that expanded across clubs, federations, and high-performance sailing environments. Her work has included coaching youth and Olympic pathway athletes, collaborating with national programs, and contributing to the development of competitive racing teams.

Today her career combines on-the-water coaching, athlete development, coach education, and the leadership of structured sailing programs that support long-term competitive growth.

Professional Identity

Cerutti’s work combines Olympic sailing experience, international coaching, and long-term program leadership. Her approach focuses on athlete development, coach mentorship, and building competitive environments where sailors can progress from youth racing to elite international competition.

Olympic Athlete

Olympic Athlete Career

Florencia Cerutti represented Paraguay in the Laser Radial class at the 2008 Olympic Games in Beijing, becoming the first Paraguayan woman to compete in Olympic sailing.

Her Olympic campaign was the result of years of international competition in single-handed dinghy classes, where she developed the technical precision, tactical awareness, and resilience that later shaped her coaching philosophy.

Throughout her competitive career, Cerutti raced internationally across Europe and the Americas, building experience in elite regattas and major championships.

Her athletic journey includes a bronze medal at the 2003 Pan American Games and a broader competitive pathway that established the foundations for her transition into international coaching.

Current Role

Head Coach & Assistant Sailing Director

Florencia Cerutti currently serves as Head Coach and Assistant Sailing Director at Norfolk Yacht & Country Club in Virginia, United States.

In this role she oversees youth racing programs, supports athlete development pathways, and contributes to the structure and growth of competitive sailing within one of the region’s most active sailing communities.

Her responsibilities include on-water coaching, program planning, coach mentorship, regatta preparation, and the development of training environments that support both performance and long-term sailor progression.
